Students at EFREI Paris

Our academic path is shaped by experimentation, teamwork, and a strong motivation to keep learning through real projects.

Subject 1

Studying computer science at Efrei means learning how to combine solid technical fundamentals with practical execution. We approach code as both a craft and a tool for solving meaningful problems, whether the challenge is building a website, structuring data, or designing a more intuitive digital service.

Subject 2

Our work is also collaborative by nature. Through presentations, peer feedback, and shared production, we learn to explain our decisions, refine our ideas, and adapt our solutions. That balance between autonomy and teamwork is a central part of the student experience we want to reflect on this site.
